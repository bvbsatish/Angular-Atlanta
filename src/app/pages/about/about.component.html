About Us

Established by young and dynamic educationalist professional and technocrats a decade ago, Bengaluru NEET Academy is one of the premium institutions in South India, providing dedicated coaching for NEET/JEE and other prominent medical and engineering exams and the board examinations. From the humble beginning in 2007 BNA posted a consistent growth throughout the decade. The crust and trough of the seasons were the real challenge for the growth of the organization however the quality the dedication and the commitment the team has exhibited retained BNA as the leader. BNA became part of MACHLAM Innovations Pvt. Ltd., a leading joint stock technology company based at Bengaluru, in 2017.
